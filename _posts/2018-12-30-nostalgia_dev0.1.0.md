<h2> Including </h2>

* shading
  * soft shadowing
  * ambient occlusion
  * lambert diffuse shading
  * custom lightmap
* volumetric fog
  * scalable down to one sample
* temporal anti-aliasing
  * helps a lot with fog quality and performance due to temporal dithering
* custom sky
* bloom
* image based auto exposure
* wind effects
* basic ingame settings screen
